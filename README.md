# 🚚 Supply Chain Operations & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain operational data to identify delivery bottlenecks, supplier performance issues, disruption impact, operational inefficiencies, and supply risk indicators.

The analysis transforms raw operational data into business insights through Python, SQL, and Power BI to support better decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Modern supply chains face operational challenges such as:

- Delivery delays
- Supplier inconsistency
- Shipment disruptions
- Inefficient resource utilization
- Increasing operational costs
- Supply risk exposure

The organization lacked visibility into supplier performance, delivery timelines, disruption impact, and operational efficiency.

This project provides a data-driven solution to improve operational performance.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

To analyze supply chain operations and generate actionable insights to improve:

- Delivery performance
- Supplier reliability
- Disruption management
- Operational efficiency
- Demand planning
- Supply risk mitigation

# 🔍 Key Insights

### Delivery Performance
- Sea and Rail shipping showed comparatively higher delays.

### Supplier Analysis
- Supplier performance varied significantly across operations.

### Disruption Analysis
- Disruptions increased fulfilment time and operational risk.

### Revenue Trends
- Certain categories generated higher business value.

### Risk Monitoring
- Supply risk concentrated among selected suppliers.

---

# 💡 Recommendations

### Delivery
Optimize shipment planning and reduce dependency on slower modes.

### Supplier
Implement supplier scorecards and performance monitoring.

### Disruption
Build contingency plans and improve response time.

### Operations
Improve communication and optimize resource usage.

### Risk
Diversify suppliers and strengthen supply monitoring.
